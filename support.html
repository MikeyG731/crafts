Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate a price for my handmade item?

In the Products tab, select your product and navigate to the Price Calculator section. Enter your material costs, hours spent, and desired profit margin. The app will automatically calculate a recommended price for your item.

How do I track my sales?

Use the Transactions tab to record all sales. After calculating a price, you can directly save it as a transaction. All your transactions will appear in the Reports tab with detailed analytics.

Can I backup my data?

The app uses your device's storage and iCloud (if enabled) to manage data. We recommend enabling iCloud in your device settings to ensure your data syncs across devices and is backed up.

How do I set my default hourly rate?

Go to the Settings tab and adjust the "Default Hourly Rate" value. This rate will be used as the default for all new price calculations.

How do I view reports of my sales?

Navigate to the Reports tab to see comprehensive analytics of your sales, including revenue by product type, time-based trends, and transaction details. You can filter by date range and product type.
