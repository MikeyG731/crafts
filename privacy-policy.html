Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Handmade Pricing ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

When you use our App, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Data: We collect only the information you voluntarily provide, such as product details, pricing information, and transaction records.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about your mobile device, including device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the App, such as the features you use and the time spent on the App.
  • Images: We access your camera and photo library only when you choose to add photos to your products, and only with your explicit permission.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App
  • Store your product and transaction data
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities connected to our App
  • Debug to identify and repair errors
  • Protect against, identify, and prevent fraud and other illegal activity

Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally on your device and, if you've enabled iCloud, in your personal iCloud account. We do not store your personal data on our servers. Your data is protected by your device's security features and Apple's iCloud security if you choose to use iCloud syncing.

Third-Party Services

Our App uses Apple's TestFlight for beta testing and Apple's App Store for distribution. These services may collect additional information as described in their respective privacy policies.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use the App. You can delete your data at any time by deleting the App or by using the data deletion features within the App.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
